Welcome to Healthify

Your smart health tracker and daily wellness guide

1. Dashboard – Visual Health Reports

Access a beautiful, user-friendly dashboard that summarizes all your fitness data in real-time. View trends in your daily activities, monitor your progress over days, weeks, and months, and make informed decisions using interactive charts and insights. Whether it's calories, steps, or active minutes — everything is visualized for clarity and motivation

2. Full Body Tracker

Know your body inside and out. Track important body metrics like weight, BMI, muscle mass, body fat percentage, hydration levels, and more — all in one place. With smart reminders and progress indicators, the full body tracker ensures you stay consistent and aware of how your body is evolving as you work toward your fitness goals.

3. Sports Activity Tracking

Push your limits with precision. Automatically log a variety of sports activities such as running, cycling, swimming, football, yoga, and gym workouts. Get detailed breakdowns including distance, pace, heart rate, and calories burned. This helps you analyze your performance and fine-tune your workouts for better results..

4. Calories & Step Counter

Track every move, burn every calorie. Stay active with a real-time calorie counter and step tracker that works seamlessly throughout your day. Set daily targets, view historical trends, and receive motivational nudges when you're close to hitting your goals. It’s the perfect companion to keep you accountable and energetic.

4. AI-Powered Fitness Chat

Your personal fitness coach, 24/7. Have a question about workouts, diets, or health goals? Ask our AI fitness assistant anything — from how to build muscle or lose fat to what meals are best before a workout. The AI provides personalized suggestions, routines, and reminders, making fitness guidance accessible anytime you need it.
